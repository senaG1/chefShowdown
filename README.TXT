PROJECT TITLE: Chef Showdown
DATE: November 22, 2025

AUTHORS: Cayden Chan, Jiayu Chen, Sena Godek, Oscar Ho, Isabel Powell, Grace Tao

SUMMARY: Welcome to the ultimate chef showdown between the famous Red and Blue Chef Cohens! Only one is qualified to recieve Masterchef Jordan Ramsey's prized chef hat. Each restaurant will arrange a unique team of their own to win the hearts of their reviewers. Between the unpredictable karens and famous influencers, the customer ratings will fully influence the winner of this competition. Over the course of 3 days, which McCohen's will reign supreme?

USER INSTRUCTIONS: Press START to begin the chef showdown between Chef Red Cohen and Chef Blue Cohen. In Settings, select the number of chefs for each team, and the starting cash for each team. Then, select one of the four unique chefs to add to the Red or Blue Restaurant based on the number of chefs chosen earlier. When fully decided, click "PLAY" to start the simulation!
